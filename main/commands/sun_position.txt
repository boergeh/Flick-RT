
  flick sun_position <command> <command_specific_argument>...

Parameters:

  <command> [command_specific_argument]... 

    Select among the following commands with corresponding parameters:

    'distance' <year> <month> <day> <UTC_hour> <minutes> <seconds>

    Gives Sun-Earth distance in astronomical units.
    
    'zenith_angle' <year> <month> <day> <UTC_hour> <minutes> <seconds>
    <latitude> <longitude>

    Gives solar zenith angle in degrees, where 'latitude' and
    'longitude' should also be given in degrees.

    'azimuth_angle' <year> <month> <day> <UTC_hour> <minutes>
    <seconds> <latitude> <longitude>

    Gives solar azimuth angle in degrees, where 'latitude' and
    'longitude' should also be given in degrees.

Examples:
    
  flick sun_position distance 2023 6 21 14 58 0    

  flick sun_position zenith_angle 2023 6 21 14 58 0 60.39 5.32

  flick sun_position azimuth_angle 2023 6 21 14 58 0 60.39 5.32


Remember: unless other units are explicitly stated, Flick is always
using SI units (m, kg, s) for input and output of physical values, see
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/International_System_of_Units.
