flick iop <parameters>  -  inherent optical property spectrum

parameters:

  pure_water {type} <from wavelength> <to wavelength>
  <number of wavelengths> [tempeature] [salinity in psu]

    where {type} = absorption_length, scattering_length, or
    refractive_index.

    Remember that unless other units are explicitly stated, Flick
    always uses SI units,
    https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/International_System_of_Units.

example:

  flick iop pure_water absorption_length 400e-9 700e-9 10 273 35



Examples:

flick list --pure_water

flick list --precision function.txt 4 4

      List tabulated x and y values in function.txt with 4 significant
      figures in both x and y.


flick list --xy function.txt

      List tabulated x and y vales in function.txt without header text.
